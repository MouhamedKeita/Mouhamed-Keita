BRAZIL

BRAZIL, a vast country in South America, extends from the Amazon basin in the north to vineyards and Iguaçu Falls in the south. The capital city is Brasília but the most know city is Rio. The city of Rio de Janeiro, characterized by its 38-metre-high statue of Christ the Redeemer at the top of Corcovado, is famous for its busy beaches of Copacabana and Ipanema, as well as for its huge lively carnival, with parades of floats, flamboyant costumes, and samba music and dance.
